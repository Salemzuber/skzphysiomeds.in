About Us

No matter what, we always try to get to the root cause of an issue—and fix it for good.

SKZ physiotherapy focuses on Torticollis, developmental delay, strains and sprains, and fractures. When we work with a patient, we initially perform a detailed assessment of the musculoskeletal pain and dysfunction. Through education, goal setting, manual therapy, dry needling, and exercise-based programming, we’ll do our best to help you succeed through a pain-free life.
